2019 Rowland HTML Website Competition

What is the competition?

Participants will use HTML to create a web page. The pages will be judged by the Programming Club officers and Mr. Kim. The winner of this competition will receive a $25 gift card to any location.

Design Your Own Page

You can make your page look however you want, using whatever code you want.

Chance to Win A Gift Card

The person who designs the best website will be awarded a $25 gift card of his/her choice!

Good Luck!
